# Getting Started with Minimalism

Minimalism isn't about having nothing. It's about having exactly what you need.

## The Core Principles

1. **Intentionality** - Every element should have a purpose
2. **Clarity** - Remove what obscures the essential
3. **Restraint** - Resist the urge to add more

&gt; "Perfection is achieved not when there is nothing more to add, but when there is nothing left to take away." — Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

## In Design

When building interfaces:

- Use whitespace deliberately
- Limit your color palette
- Choose typography carefully
- Embrace consistency
